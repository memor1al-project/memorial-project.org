Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

The Memorial Project ("we", "our", "us") is a family heritage preservation platform. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your name and email address
  • Your Google profile picture
  • A unique Google account identifier

We use this information solely for authentication and to identify you within family groups you belong to.

Family Data

You may upload or contribute family history content including genealogical records, photographs, documents, and stories. This data is stored securely and is only accessible to members of the family groups it belongs to, unless explicitly marked as public by a family administrator.

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and manage access to family groups
  • To provide AI-powered features such as chat, story generation, and document analysis using Google Gemini and other AI services
  • To generate text-to-speech audio and avatar interactions

Content you provide may be sent to third-party AI services (Google Gemini, ElevenLabs, Simli) to power these features. We do not use your data to train AI models.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is shared only with:

  • Google Cloud Platform (hosting and infrastructure)
  • AI service providers (Google Gemini, ElevenLabs, Simli) as needed to provide app features

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Google Cloud Platform with industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit and at rest, role-based access control, and regular security reviews.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Revoke Google sign-in access at any time via your Google Account settings

Cookies

We use a single session cookie for authentication purposes. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at liorp@memorial-project.org.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
